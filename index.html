Carolyn's Oil painting

MEDIUM: OIL PAINT
Girl in flower field.
This painting is inspired by black artist Kehinde Willey. Kehinde Willey is an artist famous for his naturalistic paintings of black people using both traditional and contemporary art styles. I took some of his technique into my painting, as shown with the abstract pattern on above the skies and realistic figure. The processs of painting was extensive. The duration of the process took about a month. First, I printed out my reference picture. Then I sent my image to the projector to trace the outline onto a larger piece of paper using a projector. I also traced the pattern designs i found from the online. The subject I referenced for my painting is a picture of my mom when she was young.
